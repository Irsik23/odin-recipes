Jalebi

Description

Jalebi is a quick and easy snack that was made popular in India. The snack is made by preparing a batter, fermenting the batter and then poured to form spirals in hot oil then dipped in syrup.

Ingredients

  • flour
  • turmeric
  • plain yogurt
  • water
  • baking soda
  • lemon juice
  • oil
  • sugar
  • saffron
  1. Add water, flour and turmeric in a bowl and mix to make a thick batter.
  2. Cover and ferment with plain yogurt for 24 hours.
  3. Heat oil to a medium heat.
  4. Add baking soda to your mix and put it in a squeeze bottle and squirt small spirals into the hot oil starting from the center.
  5. Add sugar and water to a pot and bring to a boil for about 2 minutes then add lemon juice and saffron.
    6. When the spirals are brown and crispy add them to the syrup and voila! You have delicious Jalebi!
