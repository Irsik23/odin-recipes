Som Tam Thai

Description

Som Tam Thai is a salad made popular in Thailand. Som Tam is a green papaya salad which usually contains beans, lime, pepper, and garlic mashed together in a mortar. In Thailand ground peanuts are added.

Ingredients

  • full papaya
  • garlic
  • red chile pepper
  • soy sauce
  • vegetable oil
  • beans
  • cherry tomatoes
  • bean sprouts
  • basil
  • sugar
  • roasted peanuts

Steps

  1. Peel and grate the papaya with a large grater.
  2. Add the garlic, chile pepper, fish sauce, oil, and sugar in a food processor to create a chile dressing.
  3. Add the shredded papaya, cherry tomatoes, basil and bean sprouts to a large salad bowl. Add the chile dressing and beans and toss.
  4. Add crushed roasted peanuts to the top and serve your delicious Tom Tam Thai salad!
