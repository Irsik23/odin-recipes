Pho

Description

Pho, also pronounced 'fuh' is a very popular soup in Asia. Pho originated in Vietnam and consists of rice noodles in broth with thin cuts of beef or chicken. You can also add herbs, vegetables and sauces, such as onion, basil, and sriracha, if you prefer.

Ingredients

  • rice noodles
  • beef strips
  • beef broth
  • fish sauce
  • sugar
  • ginger
  • onion
  • basil
  • sriracha

Steps

  1. Add the beef broth, beef, fish sauce, sugar, ginger, onion and basil to a saucepan and bring to a low boil then reduce heat to a simmer for about 30 minutes.
  2. Bring a pot of water to a boil and and add the rice noodles. Cook for about 30 seconds and add to a bowl.
  3. Pour your broth on top, add sriracha and you now have a delicious bowl of Pho.
